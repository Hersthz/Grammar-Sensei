What you get

🖍️

In-sentence highlight

The detected grammar is highlighted right inside the sentence, color-coded by JLPT level so you see exactly where it is.

🔊

Listen (TTS)

Tap the speaker on any sentence or example to hear native Japanese pronunciation — handy when romaji and real sound differ.

📤

Export to Anki / CSV

Send your saved grammar to Anki (ready-to-import cards) or CSV for Excel — keep studying outside the browser.

🧠

On-device AI fallback

When a pattern isn't in the local database, Gemini Nano can explain it — entirely on your device, no network needed.

Privacy Defaults

  • Local grammar matching is the default.
  • On-device AI (Gemini Nano) runs on your machine and sends nothing over the network.
  • Hover mode is off until you enable it.
  • Cloud AI is off until you configure an endpoint, accept consent, and click Ask AI.
  • Grammar Sensei avoids private input fields and does not scan pages continuously.
